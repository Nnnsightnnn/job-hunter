## üìñ How to Use

### First Time: Set Up Your Profile

1. Click **Profile** at the top
2. Fill in your name, email, phone, location
3. Write a short summary about yourself
4. Add your skills (separated by commas)
5. Click **Save Profile**

Then click **Edit Work Experience** and add your past jobs:
- Job title and company
- Start and end dates  
- Your accomplishments (one per line)

**Tip:** Add EVERYTHING. The AI will pick the best parts for each job!

### Finding Jobs

1. Click **Search** at the top
2. Type what kind of job you want (e.g., "Product Manager")
3. Enter your city or check "Remote only"
4. Click **Search Jobs**

### Applying to Jobs

1. Click **Jobs** to see what you've found
2. Click on a job you like
3. Click the big green **"Create Tailored Resume"** button
4. Wait about 30-60 seconds
5. Click **Download PDF**
6. Use that resume to apply!

### Tracking Your Applications

- Click status buttons to mark jobs as Applied, Interviewing, etc.
- The home page shows your progress

## üí° Tips for Better Results

**When adding your experience:**
- Use numbers: "Managed team of 5" not "Managed a team"
- Start with action words: Led, Created, Improved, Managed
- Include tools you know: Excel, Salesforce, Slack, etc.

**When searching for jobs:**
- Try different job titles
- Search both with and without location
- Check back daily for new postings
